My Role

As the lead visual designer, I was responsible for creating an immersive web experience that would showcase Neptune Studio's capabilities and attract new clients. I worked closely with the studio team to understand their brand and translate it into a compelling digital presence.

Studio Vision

Neptune Studio needed a digital presence that would:

  • Showcase their state-of-the-art equipment
  • Highlight their experienced engineers
  • Create an immersive experience for potential clients
  • Reflect the creative energy of the studio
  • Make booking sessions easy and intuitive

Visual Design

We created a dark, atmospheric design that reflects the studio's professional environment while maintaining an approachable, creative feel.

Deep blacks and blues create the studio atmosphere
