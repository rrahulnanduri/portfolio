My Role

As the lead designer, I was responsible for the complete digital experience including brand identity, user interface design, and e-commerce optimization. I worked closely with the Hudson Wilder team to understand their vision and translate it into a compelling digital presence.

Challenge

Hudson Wilder needed a digital presence that would:

  • Showcase the craftsmanship of their glassware
  • Create an intuitive shopping experience
  • Reflect the premium nature of their products
  • Work seamlessly across all devices
  • Build brand recognition and trust

Brand Identity

We developed a sophisticated brand system that emphasizes quality, elegance, and attention to detail. The visual language draws inspiration from the glassmaking process itself.

Typography

Clean, modern sans-serif with elegant serif accents for headlines

Color Palette

Neutral tones with warm gold accents to reflect the glassmaking process

Photography

High-contrast product shots that highlight the glassware's clarity and form

E-commerce Features

Product Gallery

360-degree product views with zoom functionality to showcase glassware details

Smart Filtering

Advanced filtering by material, style, and use case to help customers find the perfect piece

Size Guide

Interactive size comparison tool to help customers choose the right glassware

Care Instructions

Detailed care guides for each product to ensure longevity and customer satisfaction

User Experience

We focused on creating an intuitive shopping experience that makes it easy for customers to discover and purchase glassware that fits their lifestyle.

Intuitive Navigation

Clear categorization and search functionality

Mobile-First Design

Optimized for mobile shopping with touch-friendly interactions

Fast Loading

Optimized images and code for quick page loads

Results

45% increase in conversion rate

Improved user experience led to higher purchase completion

60% faster page load times

Optimized performance improved user satisfaction

30% increase in mobile sales

Mobile-first approach captured more mobile shoppers
